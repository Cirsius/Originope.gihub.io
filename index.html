      After finishing the true ending:

      Delusion of Zero

        A short story monologue set 18 months before the events of Chaos;Head NoAH.
        This is also available in-game with an updated translation as part of the Overhaul Patch after finishing the true ending.

      Chaos;Gate

        A short story featuring an appearance from Steins;Gate's protagonist Rintaro Okabe. Takes place during Chapter 7: Psychopath.

      Chaos;Head Love Chu Chu! (fan translation coming soon)

        A fandisk visual novel that acts as a direct sequel to Chaos;Head NoAH.
        A fan translation by the Committee of Zero team is currently in development.


    Buy on Steam | Download improvement patch | Guide


      Steins;Gate Elite (improvement patch coming soon)

        An updated re-release of Steins;Gate that uses footage from the anime adaptation instead of traditional character sprites and illustrations.
        Only recommended for replays. First-time readers are strongly advised to choose the original version over Elite.

      After finishing the true ending:

      Holy Day of the Calamitous Birth

        A novella that acts as a direct sequel to the Steins;Gate visual novel.

      Egoistic Poriomania

        An anime OVA episode that takes place after Steins;Gate in the anime adaptation's own separate continuity.

      Load Region of Déjà Vu

        A movie that takes place after Egoistic Poriomania in the anime adaptation's own separate continuity.

      A Posteriori Existence

        A drama CD that acts as a prequel to Load Region of Déjà Vu.

      Steins;Gate: Variant Space Octet (Translation patch)

        A retro-themed text adventure game set a month after the events of Steins;Gate.

      Steins;Gate: My Darling's Embrace (improvement patch coming soon / Guide)

        A romantic comedy-themed spinoff set in the peaceful Delta attractor field.

      Babel of the Grieved Maze

        A drama CD with manga and light novel adaptations that follows Kurisu during Chapter 10: Paradox Meltdown.

      Hyde of the Dark Dimension

        A drama CD that takes place in the Gamma attractor field, repurposed from what was originally a Moeka side route that was cut from the visual novel.

      Rebellion of the Missing Ring

        A manga that follows a part of Steins;Gate's events from Suzuha's perspective, as well as exploring some of her backstory.

      The Distant Valhalla

        A short story that takes place in the Alpha attractor field, following Okabe and Kurisu after they are captured by SERN.

      Braunian Motion of Love and Hate

        A prequel manga that explores the backstory of Yuugo "Mr. Braun" Tennouji and how he came to own his building.

      Amity of Budding Reminiscence (untranslated)

        A short story that shows how Okabe and Daru first met and became friends.

      Science Adventure Series Maniacs (untranslated)

        A guidebook for Chaos;Head and Steins;Gate.

      After finishing the true ending:

      Winter Sky Rocket

      Project Pleiades (untranslated)

        A prequel light novel that focuses on Subaru Hidaka.

      Maps, the Rain, and Macarons

        A short story that follows Toshiyuki Sawada during Phase 9: Exosphere Breaker, as well as exploring some of his backstory.

      The Unpublished Memoirs of Senomiya Misaki

        A prequel light novel with a manga adaptation that explores Misaki Senomiya's backstory.

      Dream Seeker (untranslated)

      Steins;Gate: Linear Bounded Phenogram (improvement patch coming soon)

        A spinoff consisting of eleven short stories written by various guest writers, most of which focus on different characters from Steins;Gate's cast.
        This has been placed here instead of in the Steins;Gate section because two of the stories contain Robotics;Notes references that can be considered spoilers.

      Tomorrow in the Box

        An interlinked crossover story featuring the protagonists of all 3 previous mainline games. Can be read at any time once Chaos;Head NoAH,
        Steins;Gate         and Robotics;Notes Elite have all been finished.

      After finishing the true ending:

      The Memoirs of a Certain Wrong-Sider (untranslated)

        A light novel retelling of Chaos;Child from multiple different perspectives, primarily, Nono Kurusu's.

      Wish

        A drama CD that follows the culprit as they ponder over their motive for committing the Return of the New Generation Madness murders.

      Slumbering Fools

        A drama CD that explores Mio Kunosato's backstory.

      Children's Collapse

        A prequel manga series that further explores Mio Kunosato's backstory, as well as her friendship with Steins;Gate's Kurisu Makise.

      Children's Collapse: First Time Going Out

        A short story set between chapters 3 and 4 of Children's Collapse.

      Chaos;Child Love Chu Chu!! (fan translation coming soon)

        A fandisk visual novel that acts as a direct sequel to Chaos;Child.
        A fan translation by the Committee of Zero team is currently in development.

      Children's Revive (poor translation)

        A light novel set some time after the visual novel that acts as an epilogue for the Chaos;Child portion of the Science Adventure series.
        The only English translation currently available is readable, but of poor quality.

      Dream

        A drama CD that was included with the Chaos;Child: Silent Sky anime OVA DVD and Blu-ray, taking place at some point after Chaos;Child.


    Buy on Steam | Download improvement patch | Guide


      After finishing the true ending:

      23β: Divide by Zero

        An alternate version of Episode 23 of the Steins;Gate anime adaptation, featuring a different ending that leads into Steins;Gate 0.
        This should be started immediately after finishing the Milky Way Crossing ending in the visual novel, followed by the below anime adaptation.

      Steins;Gate 0 anime adaptation

        The Steins;Gate 0 anime is not a straightforward adaptation of the visual novel, but rather an extension of it that shows the events that took place
        on the Milky Way Crossing ending's worldline that ultimately led to the execution of Operation Skuld.

      Paradise Lost in Adaptive Radiation

        A drama CD made of material from a route that was cut from the visual novel due to time constraints.

      Feast of the Valkyries 1 / 2 / 3 / 4 / 5

        A drama CD that explores how Okabe's Valkyrie group was formed on the Beta attractor field, among some other events.

      The Hashida Family's Love and Affection 1 / 2 / 3
      The Perpetually Infinite Hashida Concerto 1 / 2
      The Dog Days of Hashida Comima 1 / 2 / 3

        A trilogy of drama CDs following the adventures of Daru and his family on the Steins Gate worldline, set long after the events of the original Steins;Gate.

    Watch on Crunchyroll or Funimation

    Occultic;Nine is in an unusual situation; its canonical connection to the rest of the Science Adventure series isn't completely clear and the original light novel series
    has been left incomplete. Reading the light novels and then watching the anime adaptation immediately afterward is currently considered to be the best way to experience Occultic;Nine.
